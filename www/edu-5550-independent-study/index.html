EDU 5990: Doctorial Dissertation Seminar

Fall 2009
Dr. Frank Smith

This class continued the need to work on the presentation and content of the first three pages of my dissertation proposal.  Of particular importance was chapter two that necessitated a careful and concerted review of all pertinent research articles related to the topic of my interest.   With Dr. Smith as professor each Cohort member was able to use the skills previous acquired from the Moodle progam to  post our ideas as well as as to review and provide constructive criticism to each members dissertation proposals.  At the conclusion of this course each cohort member left with a concrete concept into their areas of interest and proposal for their dissertation having present such idea within the Moodle program and in person before fellow cohort members and Dr. Smith.
