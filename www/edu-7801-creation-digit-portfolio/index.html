EDU 7801: Creation Digit Portfolio

Spring 2008
Dr. Jonathan T. Hughes

This course involves students in the creation of a Digital Portfolio and the building of a website. Students are introduced to Microsoft Front Page and other designer websites that can be utilized in presenting a professional product. With a dissertation format as a guideline the members of the Cohort were instructed in organizing the website into five major areas including:

  • Personal background
  • Professional
  • Learner (courses taken, work completed)
  • Expert (career accomplishments)
  • Scholar (Dissertation Proposal and area of interest)

Students were able to explore a number of techniques and sharpen the skills needed to design a working website. Upon completion of the course members of the Cohort had the knowhow to begin a professional looking website and explore areas of interest that their dissertation may eventually emanate from. In addition, Cohort members were allowed the opportunity to view the websites from students who had completed the program and to ask pertinent questions and concerns in operating such a website with success.

Course Materials

  • Microsoft FrontPage 2003
  • Web servers including: Blue Host Website.com, Portals Hosting.Com, GoDaddy.com  etc.
  • Astound Templates
