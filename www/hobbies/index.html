Hobbies

"If a man insisted always on being serious, and never allowed himself a bit of fun and relaxation, he would go mad or become unstable without knowing it."

- Herodotus, c. 450 B.C.

Some of my varied interests include collecting and keeping Marine and Tropical fish. I have seven aquariums ranging in size from 6 to 125 gallons. I also built a 1200 gallon pond in my backyard where my many goldfish live year round. I have involved in this hobby from as far back as I can remember when as a youngster of about five years my father presented me with a 2 gallon bowl  and four guppies which quickly multiplied to well over 50 and there my obsession began.

I also acquired my second addition related hobby as an adult when I became interested in old wind up Victrolas. What started out with a passing fad soon developed into a buyer and seller of these beautiful reproductions of early sound. Machines of all sizes and makes from the early Edison witches hat horn to the later portable suitcase types surround my home to be brought back to life in an instant or at the request of guests or my younger daughters little friends.

Last but not least is my second love. No it's not what you think. I was able to find a number of years ago a 1966 Ford Mustang in restorable condition. After many years and a constant work in progress minus a good sum of hard earned money my red 66 turns heads wherever we go. She only sees the sun and is driven like the grand old lady that she is, with great respect and care.

There you have my time consuming interests. Vices to be sure but relatively harmless except to my wallet.
