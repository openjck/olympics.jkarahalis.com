Carrier of the Torch - My Journey

"The dawn speeds a man on his journey, and speeds him too in his work."

- Hesiod, c. 700 B.C.

Every day we live is a new journey. Each experience is an opportunity for continued growth.  I have traveled far and learned many things.  The journey, however is not yet over.

I now have embarked on my most challenging mission, to complete my Doctoral degree in education, a journey not taken lightly and the beginning of a goal I intend to accomplish.

As in the tradition of the light of the Olympic Flame which represents "the light of spirit, knowledge, and life," I begin my journey as the temporary keeper of the flame to be passed to others.  By doing so I hope to enlighten those individuals who remain in the darkness due to the absence of knowledge and understanding and in turn to extend the light so that others may also see and share to continue their journey as well.

I have also incorporated the Olympic symbol (the five interlocking rings) and union of the five  major continents that will represent not only fellow members of my cohort who originated from many of these parts of the world but the five major sections of my portfolio.  The blue ring is representative of the section that gives the reader insight to my Personal life including my family and hobbies.  The yellow ring within the Professional section will enable the viewer to review my academic and employment background.  The black ring within the Learner section incorporates all the classes, readings and assignments that I accomplished in pursuit of this doctoral program.  The green ring of the Expert section allows the reader to see my credentials I have acquired over the years.  Finally, the red ring of the Scholar section gives the reader access to my Doctoral dissertation proposal. 

My goal is to enable the reader to join me in my experiences that have created the person who I am today and the effect it has had on my personal and professional goals.
